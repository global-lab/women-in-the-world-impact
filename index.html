The Impact of Interactive Qualifying Projects on Women Around the World

WPI students have helped improve the lives of women and girls for over 20 years in 14 countries through 90+ Interactive Qualifying Projects (IQPs). This visualization aims to show the extent of fields in which our IQPs have made a difference for women.

To interact with the tree, click on nodes to learn more about that category, click on subcategories to get a list of all respective IQPs, scroll to pan, and click and drag to move the tree.
